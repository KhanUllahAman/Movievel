Mission: Impossible is an action espionage film directed by Brian DePalma. The plot follows Ethan Hunt (Cruise) and his mission to uncover the mole who has framed him for the murders of his entire IMF team. It was released on May 23rd, 1996. It is the first film in the Mission: Impossible Cinematic Universe.
